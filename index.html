Swami Vivekananda
  • Author
  • Philosopher
  • Indian Hindu Monk
Swami Vivekananda was one of the most popular Hindu monks and spiritual leaders of India. He was a perfect blend of classic Indian text and modern western thoughts. He died at the age of 39 in Bengal (RIP). His most known books are:
  1. Complete Works of Swami Vivekananda
  2. Raja-Yoga
  3. Karma Yoga: The Yoga of Action
